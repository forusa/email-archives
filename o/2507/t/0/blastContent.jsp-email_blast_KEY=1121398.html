Hi, Friends.  Last week, I wrote you about the Drop Beats Not Bombs tour events in Chicago. Unfortunately, there was an error in the calendar. Below is the entire corrected message so you can share it with friends and other e-mail lists. 

On the heels of this exciting election season, the Fellowship of Reconciliation in partnership with the Not Your Soldier project launching the Drop Beats Not Bombs tour - and we're coming to Chicago for events this week!

The tour's purpose is to support military resistance through creative action, which we're going to need regardless of who's in office. The tour features Paula Galeano, a Conscientious Objector from Colombia, and Detroit based artist Invincible, who resists through amazing political hip-hop. FOR & Not Your Soldier staff will lead workshops on Conscientious Objection and Art and Action, and will reach out to local organizers to connect & resource resistance to militarism through the Not Your Soldier project.

Please check out the itinerary (online and below) and RSVP for events you would like to attend.  If you can't be involved but want to support FOR's work, please consider making a financial contribution to help cover our costs.

Thanks for all of your support!!

Peace,
Maryrose Dolezal, Fellowship of Reconciliation
