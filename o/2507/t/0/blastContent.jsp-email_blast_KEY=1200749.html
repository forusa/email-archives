Dear Friends,

Tomorrow, December 15, FOR Executive Director Mark Johnson as well as newly-Austin-based FOR Program Director Susana Pimiento will meet with local FOR members, supporters, and allies in Austin, and we'd love for you to be there!

Details about tomorrow afternoon's event:

Central Texas FOR chapter meeting with Mark Johnson & Susana Pimiento
Hosted by Charlie Jackson, coordinator of Texans for Peace
4:00 p.m.
Episcopal Seminary of the Southwest
Seminary Library (Black Room)
606 Rathervue Place, Austin TX 78705
RSVP to Susana Pimiento by e-mail or by phone at 512.542.1769.

Mark and Susana will discuss FOR's vision for demilitarizing life and land across the globe. They will review FOR programs for the coming year, including our international peace work in Colombia, Afghanistan, and Iran, our efforts toward domestic racial and social justice, and our vision of supporting the work of FOR chapters and affiliates around the United States.

All are welcome to join us, so please invite your friends and family as well!

Ethan Vesely-Flad
Communications Director
