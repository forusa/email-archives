Dear Friends,

Next Tuesday, December 21, FOR Executive Director Mark Johnson will meet with local FOR members, supporters, and allies in two gatherings in Claremont and Riverside, and we'd love for you to be there!

Details about the events:

Pilgrim Place meeting with Mark Johnson
1:15 p.m.
Porter Conference Room
625 Mayflower Place
Claremont, CA 91711
RSVP to Tom & Donna Ambrogi by e-mail or via phone at 909.625.2558.

Inland Communities FOR chapter potluck supper with Mark Johnson
6:00 p.m.
Home of Linda & Michael Dunn
250 East Blaine Street
Riverside, CA 92507
RSVP to Linda Dunn by e-mail or via phone at 951.682.5364, or to Marilyn Sequoia by e-mail.

Mark will discuss FOR's vision for demilitarizing life and land across the globe. He will review FOR programs for the coming year, including our international peace work in Colombia, Afghanistan, and Iran, our efforts toward domestic racial and social justice, and our vision of supporting the work of FOR chapters and affiliates around the United States.

All are welcome, so please invite your friends and family as well!
