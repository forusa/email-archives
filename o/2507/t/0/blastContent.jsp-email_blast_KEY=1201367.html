Friends,

As the White House assembles its budget for next year, we want the voice of peace from you and your community to be heard!

That's why we're offering bulk orders of postcards directed to President Obama, that urge "No U.S. Guns in Latin America," part of a new FOR campaign to demilitarize U.S. policy in Latin America.

Order your postcards to President Obama today!

Our Latin American partners have called on FOR to work with them to bring home the U.S. military troops and bases in their countries. This militarism takes resources away from social programs at home while contributing to war and rights abuses.

As Washington considers cutting the federal budget, military bases and "aid" in Latin America are an important place to start, and these postcards are a great way to engage your community in discussion.

The cost of these color postcards is only $11 for 200 cards ($8 for 100 cards; $25 for 500 cards), which covers our printing and shipping costs.

Soon we will be contacting you about further action to demilitarize life and land. For now, please join us in organizing our desires for funding human needs, not more guns in Latin America.

Order your postcards online by secure credit or debit card purchase.

Or request your order by email to action@forusa.org and send check to: FOR, 436 14th St. #409, Oakland, CA 94612.

We look forward to working with you on the campaign to demilitarize US policy in the hemisphere!
