Dear Friends,

Today, FOR Executive Director Mark Johnson begins a three-week speaking and dialogue tour across the Midwest and Mid-Atlantic. Mark will drive more than 1,500 miles in order to meet face-to-face with hundreds of FOR members and supporters. We hope you'll be among them!

Mark will discuss FOR's vision for demilitarizing life and land across the globe. He'll review FOR programs for the coming year, including our international peace work in Colombia, Afghanistan, and Iran, our efforts toward domestic racial and social justice, and our vision of supporting the work of FOR chapters and affiliates around the United States.

Listed below are communities Mark will be visiting. To receive details about these events as they are finalized, be sure we have your city, state and zip code on file.
