Dear Friends,

Tomorrow, January 29, FOR Executive Director Mark Johnson will be the special guest speaker at the Arkansas Coalition for Peace & Justice annual membership meeting and potluck dinner in Little Rock, and we'd love for you to be there!

Details about the event:

Mark Johnson hosted by the Arkansas Coalition for Peace & Justice
6:30 PM, Saturday, Jan. 29
Unitarian Church of Little Rock
1818 Reservoir Road
Little Rock, AR (map and directions)

This is a free evnet, but please RSVP by emailing acpj@att.net, calling (501) 944-9297, or RSVP on Facebook.

FOR Executive Director Mark Johnson is on a three-week speaking and dialogue tour across the Midwest and Mid-Atlantic. Mark will drive more than 1,500 miles in order to meet face-to-face with hundreds of FOR members and supporters. We hope you’ll be among them!

Mark will discuss FOR’s vision for demilitarizing life and land across the globe. He’ll review FOR programs for the coming year, including our international peace work in Colombia, Afghanistan, and Iran, our efforts toward domestic racial and social justice, and our vision of supporting the work of FOR chapters and affiliates around the United States.

All are welcome to join us, so please invite your friends and family as well!

Ethan Vesely-Flad
Communications Director
