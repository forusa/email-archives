Dear Friends,

Tomorrow afternoon, February 12, FOR Executive Director Mark Johnson will speak at a lunch with Ohio Valley PEACE, and we’d love for you to be there!

Details about the event:

Lunch with FOR director Mark Johnson and Ohio Valley PEACE
12:00 PM, Saturday, Feb. 12
Ohio Valley Unitarian Universalist Church
66166 Kirkwood Heights Rd.
Bellaire, Ohio (view map and get directions)

This is a free event, and the public is invited. Please RSVP by contacting patnjake@sprynet.com or 304.242.8873, or registering on Facebook.

FOR Executive Director Mark Johnson is on a three-week speaking and dialogue tour across the Midwest and Mid-Atlantic. Mark is driving more than 1,500 miles in order to meet face-to-face with hundreds of FOR members, supporters, and allies. We hope you’ll be among them!

Mark will outline FOR’s vision for demilitarizing life and land around the globe, which aligns our work in Latin America and the Middle East with the roots of the "triple evils" that Martin Luther King, Jr. named: our country's militarism, materialism/poverty, and racism. He'll also discuss our ongoing relationships with local FOR chapters and affiliates.

All are welcome, so please invite your friends and family!

Ethan Vesely-Flad
Communications Director
