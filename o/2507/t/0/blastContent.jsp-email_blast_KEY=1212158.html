Dear Friends,

Tomorrow and Thursday, FOR Executive Director Mark Johnson will give two presentations and discussions on the peace movement and interfaith peace work.

Both events are open to the public, and we invite you and your community to join us for these conversations!

Rethinking How We Move Forward As A Peace Movement

Wednesday, April 20, 7:00 PM
Central Presbyterian Church
4th & Kentucky Streets, Louisville
View map and get directions, or view the event on Facebook

Mark Johnson will reflect on what he has gleaned from visiting with dozens of peace groups during his recent trip to over twenty states across the United States. A free event, tax-deductible contributions will be joyously received.

For more details, please call Jean Edwards at (502) 458-8056.

Third Thursday Lunch: Demilitarizing Life and Land

Thursday, April 21, 11:30 AM
Rudyard Kipling Restaurant
422 West Oak Street, Louisville
View map and get directions, or view the event on Facebook

Mark's presentation will focus on the Fellowship of Reconciliation's new campaign to Demilitarize Life and Land, as it relates to the national rallies that occurred on April 9 opposing the wars in Iraq and Afghanistan. He will outline the work FOR does, aligning programs in Latin America and the Middle East with the roots of the "triple evils" that Martin Luther King, Jr. named -- militarism, materialism/poverty, and racism.

Buffet lunch tickets are available for $6 by calling Jean Edwards at (502) 458-8056. Please call ahead to reserve your seat!

Sponsored by the Louisville Chapter of the Fellowship of Reconciliation and by Interfaith Paths to Peace.

We look forward to seeing you at both of these events!

Ethan Vesely-Flad
Communications Director
