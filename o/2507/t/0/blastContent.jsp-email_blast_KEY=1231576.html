Friends,

You are cordially invited to FOR's annual winter solstice celebration and open house on Dec. 18! Great music, food, conversation and community! Children always welcome. Feel free to come for some or all of the afternoon!

Sunday, Dec. 18
1:30 PM to 5:30 PM
FOR, 521 N. Broadway, Nyack, NY
Get driving directions | View on Facebook | Share on Twitter

Musical performances by Naaz Hosseini & Friends, Paul Speziale and the NJ Jazz Ensemble, Regret the Hour, Answer the Muse, Joey Levine, Brooke Smokelin, Lisa Sokolov and The Lower Hudson Valley Youth Chorus under the direction of Jared Rosenblum.

Suggested donation of $10, and in the spirit of the season, please bring a dish, savory or sweet, to share. Beverages will be provided -- alcohol and smoke free. If you have any questions, just reply to this email.

Snow or no snow, the party goes on -- see you there!

Made possible, in part, with funds from the Community Arts Grants program of the Arts Council of Rockland and the Decentralization Program of the New York State Council on the Arts.
