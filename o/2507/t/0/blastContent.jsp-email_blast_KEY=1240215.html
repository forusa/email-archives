Friends,

Pop music superstar Lady Gaga told Nicholas Kristof of The New York Times last week, "I have a voice in the universe, and I want to do everything I can to become an expert in social justice and hope I can make a difference and mobilize young people to change the world."

When one of the best-selling music artists of all time announces she is focused on becoming an "expert in social justice," we applaud!

You may not have the resources to launch a multimillion-dollar initiative to empower youth, as Lady Gaga has. But you can act "in concert" with her to pursue lasting social change!

"No justice, no harmony," sings Lady Gaga in a song on her Born This Way album. For almost a century, the Fellowship of Reconciliation has organized to build a just and harmonious world, bringing people together to explore the power of love and truth for resolving human conflict.

Join the Fellowship of Reconciliation for free today and make a lifetime commitment to human rights, ending war, and racial and economic justice. Membership in FOR is accomplished through a simple act: just read and sign our Statement of Purpose.

Your tax-deductible donation helps FOR to continuing building the Beloved Community today and for the years to come.

In peace,

Mark C. Johnson, Ph.D.
Executive Director
