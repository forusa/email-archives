Friends,

The Interfaith Peace Walk is a walking dialogue between Jews, Christians, Muslims, Buddhists, Hindus, Sikhs, Wiccans and others.

The theme this year is "Stewards of the Earth, Stewards of Each Other, Stewards of Peace." Music, prayers, rituals and wisdom teachings will be featured at three houses of worship along the walk route. Refreshments and fellowship will follow at the final stop.

The Fellowship of Reconciliation is pleased to announce that this ninth annual peace walk will feature a special greeting by long-time FOR interfaith leader Rabbi Lynn Gottlieb.

Philadelphia Interfaith Walk for Peace and Reconciliation
Sunday, April 29, 2:00 PM
Temple Beth Zion-Beth Israel
300 S 18th Street, Philadelphia, PA
Get directions to this event or view on Facebook

A pre-walk gathering will occur at 12:30 pm at Al-Aqsa Mosque and Islamic Center, 1501 Germantown Avenue. A bus will be available to take participants to the walk's starting location, Temple Beth Zion-Beth Israel, at 1:20 pm for the walk's 2:00 pm start time. The bus will follow the walk route for those who are unable to walk the distance and will also bring participants back to Al-Aqsa Islamic Center at the walk's end.
