Dear Friends,

Rev. Patti Ackerman, FOR Freeman Fellow and the UN Representative for FOR and the International Fellowship of Reconciliation, will be speaking at two events next week on countering gender-based violence around the world.

We hope you join us for one or both of these events!

Thursday: Gender-Based Violence & Religion: An Intersectional Perspective

View event details
Next Thursday, March 7, 8:30 AM
United Nations Church Center
Hardin Room, 11th Floor, 777 United Nations Plaza (get directions)

Religious sexual fundamentalisms are the increasing source of extreme violences against women, girls, and LGBT people across the globe. Sexual fundamentalists seek to control reproductive rights, contraception, comprehensive sexual education and the LGBT right to exist, as they enforce marriage and oppressive traditions. This UN CSW workshop will look at the way religious violences are being institutionalized at the UN, and how the movement is hijacking the international agenda of women’s rights and sexual rights, putting 50 years of women’s organizing at the UN at risk. Read more about this event.

Free and open to the public. Sponsored by FOR, IGLHRC, and CONNECT NYC.

Saturday: 1 in 4 No More! Gender-Based Violence Analyses

View event details
Next Saturday, March 9, 4:30 PM
United Nations Church Center
Hardin Room, 11th Floor, 777 United Nations Plaza (get directions)

Five outstanding students will present cutting edge work on women facing violence across the globe. Rev. Ackerman and two other faculty members of City College will also present. Please come support these young feminist activist/scholars working to analyze contemporary problems of gender-based violence. Read more about this event.

Free and open to the public. Sponsored by FOR and the Women’s Studies and International Studies Programs of The City College of NY.
