Friends,

Supporters and friends of the Fellowship of Reconciliation in Connecticut are invited to join Martha DiGiovanni, past FOR National Council member, for a community forum on gun violence prevention this week:

Our Community Reflects on Gun Violence
Wed., March 20, 7:00 to 8:30 PM
Manchester Community College - SBM Charitable Foundation Auditorium, AST Building
60 Bidwell Street, Manchester
Get directions to this event.

“As an elementary school teacher, I’m particularly interested in how our schools can protect our students from guns,” says DiGiovanni. “Sandy Hook struck me deeply and I don’t want to see the same thing happen here.”

The forum will offer the audience the opportunity to hear different responses from community leaders along with their visions of how to address the crisis. The event will consider gun violence from four different perspectives, religious, political, educational, and law enforcement.

For more information contact John Ryan: (860) 643-2403 x14 or jryan@saintbridget.com.
