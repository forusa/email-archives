Friends,

Hudson Valley FOR invites you to an Earth Day event at the FOR headquarters in Nyack about stewardship of the earth and the environment.

Join us for a wonderful day of festivities to pay tribute to Mother Earth. Designed for the young and old alike -- music, art, educational workshops, speakers from ecology groups, organic food, raffles and fun!

Additionally, the Rockland Coalition to End the New Jim Crow invites you to a community discussion in Spring Valley about navigating police relations and the criminal justice system.

We hope to see you at one or both events!

Staying Safe, Staying Strong: Navigating Police Relations and the Criminal Justice System
