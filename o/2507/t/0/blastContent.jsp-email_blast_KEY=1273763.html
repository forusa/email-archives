Friends,

The Fellowship of Reconciliation is hiring a Special Events Coordinator and Peace House Host to care for and maintain FOR's headquarters in Upper Nyack, NY.

Reporting to FOR's Director of Operations, this important national staff position maintains a calendar of programs and space use by non-programmatic and non-tenant groups and individuals; negotiates contracts for rentals by outside users; handles all aspects of planning for annual and quarterly events; coordinates with building maintenance staff, vendors and volunteers; supports the National Council meetings; and assists with logistics for national program training events and delegations at Shadowcliff and at remote locations. Learn more about the position or contact Heshi Gorewitz to submit a cover letter and resume.
