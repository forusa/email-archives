Friends,

You have two opportunities in the next month to connect with fellow peacebuilders, including FOR worldwide staff, in the Pacific Northwest. Francesco Candelari, the International Coordinator of IFOR (based in The Netherlands), will travel to Washington State this July to attend and speak at the FOR regional conference at Seabeck near Seattle, as well as the Baptist Peace Fellowship of North America peace camp in Spokane.

FOR-USA national staff and council members from California, Georgia, and Illinois are also scheduled to attend and offer workshops at the BPFNA conference in Spokane.
