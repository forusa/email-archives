Follow along beginning at 11:00 AM Eastern/8:00 AM Pacific

  • Check MSNBC online to see if they broadcast the event live online. The recorded broadcast will likely be available online later in the day.
  • Other cable channels, and local broadcast channels, may broadcast the full event or excerpts.
  • If you're in D.C., please join us at the event or afterwards for an informal gathering.
