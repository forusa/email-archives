Friends,

Join the Minnesota Fellowship of Reconciliation on Sunday, Nov. 3rd from 2:00 to 5:00 PM for its annual fall conference, featuring music, refreshments and fellowship.

This year’s Minnesota FOR Peacemaker of the Year Award will be given to Bishop Emeritus Lowell Erdahl, and he will also serve as the event’s featured speaker. Bishop Erdahl is the retired bishop of the St. Paul Area Synod of the Evangelical Lutheran Church in America. He has also served as a parish priest, a seminary professor, and has authored several books.
