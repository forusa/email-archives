Fellowship of Reconciliation


Friends,

You are warmly invited to FOR's annual Winter Solstice house concert and Nyack-Area Peace Award presentation this Sunday, Dec. 22!

This year's celebration will be a wonderful program of music as well as food, conversation, and community. Families with children are most welcome. Please come for some or all of the afternoon!

Sunday, Dec. 22
1:00 PM to 7:00 PM
FOR's Shadowcliff headquarters
521 N. Broadway, Nyack, NY
Get driving directions | View on Facebook | Share on Twitter

Featuring a peace award ceremony honoring this year's Nyack-area awardees, Steve and Emilia White.

We are delighted to welcome nationally-renowned musicians to this year's concert, including the Jay Ungar & Molly Mason Family Band with Mike & Ruthy, Regret the Hour, Pat Lamanna, Naaz Hosseini & Andrea Goodman, Lisa Sokolov, GDE (Tjokorda Gde Arsa Artha), and Answer the Muse, with master of ceremonies Paul Speziale.

We regret that due to a needed change in his scheduling, Pete Seeger is not able to participate in the Solstice event this year. We hope to see him at a future event and thank everyone for their support.

Suggested donations are $20 (individual), $40 (family), and $60 (Earth Angel).

In the spirit of the season, please bring a dish, savory or sweet, to share. Beverages will be provided -- alcohol and smoke free.

For more information, reply to this email or contact FOR at (845) 358-4601 ext. 32 or ext. 29. (Please note that space is limited.)

Snow or no snow, the party goes on -- see you there!

FOR's Solstice Celebration is made possible, in part, with funds from the New York State Council on the Arts (NYSCA) and the Arts Council of Rockland (ACOR), along with a generous donation by a friend of FOR.
