FOR is honored to invite you to join us at a special event featuring FOR Freeman Fellow Rev. Osagyefo Uhuru Sekou with activist and author David Hartsough next week in New York City!

From Montgomery to Ferguson

Tuesday, Dec. 9, 8:00 PM
Maryhouse Catholic Worker,
55 E. 3rd Street, New York, NY

View this event on Facebook.
View map and get directions to the event.

In celebration of his landmark memoir, Waging Peace: Global Adventures of a Lifelong Activist, David Hartsough will speak at the historic Catholic Worker auditorium with Rev. Osagyefo Uhuru Sekou, an author, pastor, and activist who has been intimately involved with the ongoing struggle in Ferguson, Missouri.

Hartsough, a civil rights movement veteran, and Rev. Sekou will compare notes on the struggle for justice in the United States of America, then and now.

Learn more about David Hartsough in Waging Nonviolence's recent profile of him.

Read Rev. Sekou's most recent essay in Al Jazeera America, "The Clergy's Place Is With the Protesters in Ferguson."

Sponsored by Waging Nonviolence and the Fellowship of Reconciliation.
