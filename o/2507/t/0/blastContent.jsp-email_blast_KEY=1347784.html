Friends,

The Fellowship of Reconciliation -- our nation's oldest interfaith peace and justice organization, as you know -- has been headquartered at Shadowcliff in Nyack since 1956.

In those 60 years, we've accumulated everything from typewriters to bird houses -- and it's time to lighten the load.

Join us at a yard sale for peace on July 16

The first phase of our project is to share some of the historic peace items we have with our community -- and help fund our ongoing work.

Summer peace sale at Shadowcliff
Saturday, July 16
10:00 AM to 4:00 PM

View this event on Facebook and invite your friends!

FOR headquarters
521 N. Broadway, Nyack, NY
Directions to Shadowcliff

Please join us on Saturday July 16 -- and please spread the word!

Items for sale include historic books, vintage electronics, furniture, knickknacks, artwork, peace movement posters and memorabilia, past publications from FOR and allies, audio and video media, household items, and so much more!

All proceeds go to support FOR's mission to organize, train, and grow a diverse peace movement.

An escorted walk-through of Shadowcliff will be offered for those interested in checking out items to be auctioned off at a later date to be announced.

Come out to meet our staff, grab a bite at the refreshments table, and take home a piece of peace movement history!

Rain or shine, I hope to see you here!
