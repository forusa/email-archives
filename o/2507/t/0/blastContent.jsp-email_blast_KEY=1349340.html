Friends,

I want to offer a heartfelt thanks for your past support for the Fellowship of Reconciliation through recurring donations.

Your financial contributions to FOR and the groups we fiscally sponsor make it possible for us to build peace. Thank you!

Recently, as you know, we launched a new website. A major part of that project was streamlining our financial service provider to a new system to reduce costs for our staff and increase usability for our supporters.

In the near future, we'll be contacting you directly to set up your donations on our new processor.

For legal reasons, we can't simply "transfer" your automatic donations to our new donation system -- you, and only you, can authorize your credit or debit card to do so. We'll guide you through this process, however, and make it as painless as possible!

If you have any questions or concerns with this process, please feel free to reply to this message. We sincerely appreciate your ongoing support!

In peace,

Jonette O'Kelley Miller, M.P.A.
